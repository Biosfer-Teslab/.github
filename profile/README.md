## 👋 About us

**Biosfer Teslab** is a European reference company in the analysis of biological samples using high-performance **Nuclear Magnetic Resonance — NMR** technology.

Founded in 2013, Biosfer Teslab is a spin-off company of:

- 🎓 **Universitat Rovira i Virgili — URV**
- 🔬 **Institut d’Investigació Sanitària Pere Virgili — IISPV**

We work at the intersection of **metabolomics, biomedical research, data science and precision health**, transforming complex biological data into meaningful and actionable knowledge.

Our purpose is to improve the understanding of metabolism, support biomedical research and contribute to better human health.

## 🔬 What we do

### 🧬 Metabolomics

We provide comprehensive metabolomic characterization of biological samples using robust, reproducible and high-throughput NMR technology.

Our platform supports the analysis of:

- 🩸 Plasma and serum
- 💧 Urine
- 🧫 Cell cultures and culture media
- 🧬 Tissues
- 🔬 Faecal samples
- 🧪 Other biological matrices

Our analyses help researchers obtain a detailed view of metabolic pathways and identify metabolic changes associated with health, disease, nutrition, treatment and lifestyle.

## 🧪 Our solutions

### Liposcale® Test

An advanced lipoprotein test designed to provide a comprehensive characterization of the lipoprotein profile.

It evaluates parameters such as:

- Lipid content
- Lipoprotein particle concentration
- Lipoprotein particle size
- Cardiovascular risk-related markers

---

### MetBalance

A metabolic health solution that combines:

- Nuclear Magnetic Resonance
- Artificial Intelligence
- Biological data analysis
- Personalized visualization

Its objective is to provide a broader understanding of metabolic status and support precision health and wellness monitoring.
